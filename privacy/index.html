Privacy Policy

At TomaNote, we value your privacy. This policy explains how your data is handled.

Data Storage

All your notes are stored locally in your browser's LocalStorage. We do not have any servers that store your data. This means:

  • Your notes never leave your device
  • We cannot access your notes
  • We do not collect any personal information
  • No cookies are used for tracking
  • No analytics or third-party tracking

No Account Required

TomaNote works completely anonymously. No email, no registration, no account needed. You are in full control of your data.

Data Security

Data saved in this notepad is stored in your browser's LocalStorage, which does not guarantee encryption.We recommend not storing sensitive or private information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
